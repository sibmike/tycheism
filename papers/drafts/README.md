The papers in this directory are **working drafts** (v6 as of 2026-04-03). They are not the final versions.

They are included in the public repository for transparency about the full scope of the Tychism trilogy:

- **Paper II — Tychism II: Trajectory Sovereignty as Epistemic Necessity** — argues that moral development instantiates the coupled-learner dynamics demonstrated in Paper I, then derives trajectory sovereignty as an epistemic necessity rather than a value preference.
- **Paper III — Tychism III: Alignment as Coupling Interference** — maps current AI alignment practices (content filtering, RLHF, Constitutional AI, recommender systems) onto the intervention operators defined in Papers I and II.

[`../paper_a_trajectory_divergence.md`](../paper_a_trajectory_divergence.md) is the only finalized paper in the repository. Drafts here will be replaced with finalized versions as they are completed.
